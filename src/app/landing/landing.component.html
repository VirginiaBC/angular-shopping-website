Welcome to My Shopping Website Demo

Are you ready to explore my shopping website and experience hassle-free online shopping? Let's get started!

How to Use My Demo:

  • Browse Products: Explore a wide range of products. You can view product details by clicking the product and add items to your cart.
  • Shopping Cart: Visit the "Cart" page to view items you've added to your cart. You can remove items or proceed to checkout.
  • Checkout: Click "Checkout" to review your order, provide shipping information, and complete your purchase.
  • Order History: Login then visit the "My Order" page to view your order history and track the status of your orders.
  • Login/Logout: You can log in or log out using the "Login/Logout" button in the navigation bar.
  • Enjoy Shopping: Explore the features, add products to your cart, and experience the convenience of online shopping with my demo.

Note: This is a demonstration website, and no real transactions will take place. Feel free to browse, add items to your cart, and enjoy the shopping experience without any actual charges.

Happy shopping!
